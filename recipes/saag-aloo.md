### Ingredients

* 4 tbsp sunflower oil
* 2 onions, finely chopped
* 4 garlic cloves, crushed
* 2 tbsp chopped ginger
* 1kg potatoes, cut into 2cm chunks
* 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
* 1 tsp black mustard seeds
* 1 tsp cumin seeds
* 1 tsp turmeric
* 500g spinach leaves

### Method

1. Parboil the potato chunks.

2. Heat sunflower oil in a large pan, add finely chopped onions, garlic and chopped ginger,
and fry for about 6 mins.

3. Stir in potato chunks, chilli flakes, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, turmeric and 1 tsp salt
and continue cooking and stirring for 5 mins more.

4. Add a splash of water, cover, and cook gently for 8-10 mins.

5. Check the potatoes are ready by spearing with the point of a knife, and if they are,
add spinach leaves and let it wilt into the pan. Take off the heat and serve.
