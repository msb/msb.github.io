title: Chicken & Couscous One-Pot
---

### Ingredients

* 3-4 (depending on size) chicken breast sliced
* 2 tsp turmeric
* 1 tbsp garam masala
* 2 tbsp sunflower oil
* 2 onions finely sliced
* 3 garlic cloves crushed
* 500ml chicken stock
* large handful whole green olives
* zest and juice 1 lemon
* 250g couscous
* small bunch flat-leaf parsley chopped

### Method

1. Season the chicken and sprinkle with chilli flakes according to taste. Arrange them on some foil and then grill for a few minutes, turning once until cooked through but not dried out. Set aside.

2. Pour the oil into the pan and then fry the onions and garlic for 8 mins until golden. Stir in the rest of the spices and cook for 1 min longer. Pour over the chicken stock and add the olives, chicken, and zest. Bring everything to the boil and then turn down the heat.

3. Cover the pan with a lid, then simmer gently for 10-15 mins until the chicken is tender. While you are doing this, put the kettle on.

4. Turn the heat down low and add the lemon juice, parsley and couscous to the pan. Place the lid back on the pan, then cook for 5 mins, stirring frequently and topping up with boiling water as required until the couscous is cooked.
